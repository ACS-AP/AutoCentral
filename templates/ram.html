Q & A

Commonly asked questions below

  • What is the average price?

    The Ram's starting MSRP sits at $33,975 for the Quad Cab model, which is an above-average starting price for the class. Crew Cab models with the short bed start at $36,675, while Crew Cab models with the long bed start at $36,975.

  • Is it a reliable vehicle?

    The Dodge Ram 1500 Reliability Rating is 3.5 out of 5.0, which ranks it 4th out of 17 for fullsize trucks. The average annual repair cost is $670 which means it has average ownership costs.

  • Is it safe?

    The Ram 1500 scored a superior rating in the vehicle-to-vehicle for the overall evaluation with the optional Advanced Safety Group or Level 1 Safety Group.

  • Does it have good MPG?

    The 2021 RAM 1500 has plenty of upgrades that make it an impressive vehicle for Pasadena drivers, including the outstanding RAM 1500 mpg available. The RAM 1500 gas mileage clocks in at 22 mpg in the city, and 32 mpg on the highway.

  • What is the average rating of this vehicle?

    The Ram Piclup has an average rating of 3.8/5 star, which is decent for a truck.
