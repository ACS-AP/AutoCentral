Q & A

Commonly asked questions below

  • What is the average price?

    The 2020 Nissan Rogue compact SUV has a Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) starting at $25,200 for a base S model, before the $1,045 destination charge. Given all its extra features, we highly recommend spending a bit more for the Rogue SV, which starts at a reasonable $26,620.

  • Is it a reliable vehicle?

    The Nissan Rogue Reliability Rating is 4.0 out of 5.0, which ranks it 13th out of 26 for compact SUVs. The average annual repair cost is $467 which means it has excellent ownership costs.

  • Is it safe?

    Yes, it's pretty safe. It's not an award winner or a top safety pick, but it did receive an average of four stars.

  • Does it have good MPG?

    Able to achieve 27 mpg city and 35 mpg highway* on Arlington roads, the 2021 Nissan Rogue gas mileage is not only exceptional by SUV standards, but the Rogue can give most sedans a run for their money as well.
