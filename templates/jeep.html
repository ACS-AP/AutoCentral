Q & A

Commonly asked questions below

  • What is the average price?

    The current average sales price of a used 2020 Jeep Wrangler is $40,777, while the average for a 2019 model is $38,625. But there is a significant exception: the 2017 Jeep Wrangler's average sales price is currently $32,103

  • Is it a reliable vehicle?

    The 2022 Wrangler has a predicted reliability score of 83 out of 100. A J.D. Power predicted reliability score of 91-100 is considered the Best, 81-90 is Great, 70-80 is Average, and 0-69 is Fair and considered below average.

  • Is it safe?

    The 2021 Jeep Wrangler earned a “Good” rating from the IIHS (Insurance Institute for Highway Safety) in most crashworthiness tests, including moderate overlap front side tests, and elements such as roof strength, head restraints and seats.

  • Does it have good MPG?

    According to the manufacturer, the newest Jeep Wrangler – the JL – gets about 22 MPG in the city and 24 on the highway. This is the rating for the Sport version of the JL. Keep in mind, there are several factors that can affect your gas mileage.

  • What is the average rating of this vehicle?

    According to RepairPal, the Jeep Wrangler has a customer Rating of 3.8 out of 5.
