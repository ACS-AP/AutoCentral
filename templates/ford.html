Q & A

Commonly asked questions below

  • What is the average price?

    How Much Does the Ford F-150 Cost? The F-150 carries a base price of $28,940, which is one of the lower starting prices in the class. SuperCab models start at $33,025, and SuperCrew models start at $36,650.

  • Is it a reliable vehicle?

    The Ford F-150 Reliability Rating is 3.5 out of 5.0, which ranks it 7th out of 17 for fullsize trucks. The average annual repair cost is $788 which means it has higher than average ownership costs.

  • Is it safe?

    The F-150 offers plenty of advanced safety equipment, but its standard headlights don't make the grade. Even still, the truck scored top rating of Good in every IIHS crash test and NHTSA five-star ratings in front, side, and overall tests and a four-star rollover rating.

  • Does it have good MPG?

    All-new F-150 with 3.5-liter PowerBoost™ V6 – the only full hybrid powertrain available in a pickup – has the best combined fuel economy for gas-powered light-duty full-size pickups with an EPA-estimated rating of 25 mpg on 4x2 models.

  • What is the average rating of this vehicle?

    The Ford F-150 is the highest rated, and most sold truck out there. Solid option with customer ratings at 4.4/5.
